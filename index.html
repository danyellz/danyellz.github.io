Privacy Policy

App: FluidDocs
Effective date: 2026-01-28

Plain-language summary: FluidDocs is designed to work offline-first. Your imported documents stay on your device. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data.

1) Who we are

This Privacy Policy describes how FluidDocs (“we”, “us”) handles information when you use the FluidDocs mobile application (the “App”).

2) Information we collect

We do not collect personal data from you.

  • No account is required.
  • No analytics or advertising identifiers are used.
  • No tracking across apps or websites.
  • No sale or sharing of personal data.

3) Documents and user content

FluidDocs lets you import documents (for example PDFs, text files, Markdown, or code files). These files and any extracted text (“User Content”) are stored locally on your device.

  • We do not upload your documents to our servers.
  • We do not access or view your documents.
  • We do not sell or share your documents.

4) On-device processing and AI features

FluidDocs may provide question answering features using on-device processing. The App is intended to answer questions based on the text available in the imported document(s).

  • Processing is designed to occur locally on your device.
  • The App is designed to function without an internet connection.

Note: If you enable optional features in the future that require network access (for example, importing from third-party storage providers), FluidDocs will only access data you explicitly choose to import.

5) Network access

FluidDocs is designed to work offline. If the App makes network requests in the future (for example, to support optional imports or to retrieve a file you select), those requests will be limited to providing the feature you requested.

  • We do not transmit your document contents to our servers.
  • We do not use network access for advertising or tracking.

6) Third-party services

FluidDocs does not include third-party advertising SDKs or analytics SDKs.

If you choose to use optional integrations (such as importing from a third-party cloud storage provider), that provider’s privacy policy will apply to their services. FluidDocs only requests access necessary to import the specific file(s) you choose.

7) Data retention

Your documents, extracted text, and question history (if enabled) are stored locally on your device until you delete them or uninstall the App.

8) Your choices and controls

  • You can delete imported documents within the App (if the feature is provided) or by removing the App.
  • You can manage device permissions in Settings (for example, file access).

9) Children’s privacy

FluidDocs is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10) Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information stored within the App. However, no method of storage is 100% secure. You are responsible for securing your device and using device passcodes/biometrics where available.

11) Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the “Effective date” above.

12) Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact:
support@yourdomain.com

This policy is intended to be consistent with App Store privacy disclosures such as “Data Not Collected” when applicable.
